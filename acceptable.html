Acceptable Use Policy

Version: 1.0 • Last Updated: July 18, 2025

Owner: Head of Information Security

Purpose

Defines responsible, secure, and lawful usage of CyberDyne Health’s systems. Supports HIPAA, ISO 27001, and GDPR compliance.

Key Highlights

  • Approved devices only; sensitive data must be encrypted
  • MFA required for remote access
  • Professional, secure email and internet practices
  • Violations must be reported to security@vyber.com
